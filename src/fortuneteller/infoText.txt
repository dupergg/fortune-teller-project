Fortune-Teller Application is an application full of wonders. There are three ways that you can receive a fortune.
1. You can ask for any type of fortune, and Madam Bridgite will do her best to guide you.
2. Tarot cards are here to guide you and give insight. Think of an object or question of concern, press a button and find your answer.
3. Ponder the orb. You can ask for a fortune, or you can just sit back and let the orb guide you. It is up to you.
